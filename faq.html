Frequently Asked Questions

What phones are my Qard products compatible with?

Most Iphones and Android phones come with NFC reading capabilities. Some Android phones will have to manually turn on the NFC reading.

Does the person scanning the NFC item need the Qard DAPP?

No DAPP needed for the end user! We display you information outside of the DAPP when being read by another device.

Will I have to program anything?

No! The wallet owner will be linked to preprogrammed items before shipped.

What can I do with my Qard Items?

We offer a variety of different useful components to the NFT community, however the two main objectives are Networking and Data Aggregation.

Are the raffle and Whitelist for the same thing?

The raffle will be for an Ultimate Pass spot with a supply of 500 and the whitelist is for the General Passes with a 9,500 supply.
