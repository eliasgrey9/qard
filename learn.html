Intro

There were some companies who were breaking into the technology of NFC Chips, but absolutely none exploring the avenue of connecting the world of NFT’s to the world of NFC chips.

Note: Information about NFC chips can be found in the “Technology” Section.


We also noticed there were different non-NFT driven companies harnessing the power of NFC chips working to charge you vast amounts of money for each product, for the monthly service, and sometimes both!


That's where Qard comes in. Our mission is to provide frequent NFT value, visual data, social interoperability, and sustainable NFC products to the community. By purchasing a one time pass you will have access to a lifetime membership, reasonably priced replacement wearables, future customization, and a strategically fast shipping.


With Qard you will have all your favorite analytics tools, social groups, NFT alerts, Exchanges, IRL conference Access, and cutting edge networking capabilities.


You may be a little confused on how everything works but rest assured, you are in the right place. In the next few sections we will take you through the details and goals of Qard. We are excited share what we’ve been working on!
Technology
A Near-Field Communication chip (NFC chip or NFC chipset) is a silicon component or Integrated Circuit (IC) that can be used in different ways, depending on the targeted application.


When connected to an appropriate antenna, an NFC chip enables short-range, wireless communication between two devices. This provides an additional layer of security, as only devices within close proximity of each other can communicate via NFC.
Application
The NFC technology was first introduced into smartphones in September 2011 and is now widely integrated into most phones, whether they are iOS or Android based.


An NFC-enabled smartphone uses an NFC controller chip connected to an NFC Antenna.


In other words, we program these chips for you to be scannable with your phone app. The end user will not need an app to view any dynamic information you wish to network.
